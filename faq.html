Your Account

Find out how you can create an account or edit your details
How do i create account?

Creating an account with us is easy. You can either sign in using your Facebook account, or fill up your details on our Sign Up page and click Create My Account.

How do I change my language settings?

You can change your preferred language under Communication Preferences in your account settings, or scroll to the footer to choose either English or Arabic as your desired language.

How do I change my password?

Sign in to your account and select Change Password in Account Overview to replace your current password with a new one. If you've forgotten your password, click on Forgot your password? on the Sign In Page for reset instructions.

Can I change my email address?

Once you've created an account, it’s not possible to change your email address as it is linked to all your account details. If you want to use a different email address, you’ll have to create a new account.

How do I add or edit my delivery information?

You can sign in to your account and select Address Book in Account Overview to add new delivery information, update or delete old ones, as well as set a default address for your next order.

What details can I view in the Order History?

The Order History section in Account Overview lets you check the status of your current order(s), access your tax invoices, print them, or re-order previously ordered items.

What are the other things I can do with my account?

When you’re signed in to your account, you can also do the following:

  • Change your subscription preferences to our newsletters and deals by selecting Communication Preferences and choosing the way ACE contacts you – either through email or SMS.
  • You can change your contact details under Personal Details in Account Overview

Returns & Refunds

Everything about our hassle-free returns & refunds
How long do I have to return an item?

You have 30 days from the date of purchase to return any item that meets our return policy.

What is the return policy?
  • We’ll refund an item(s) that is accompanied by the original proof of purchase such as your receipt or tax invoice
  • The returned item(s) must be in good and unused condition, in its original sealed packaging and with all paperwork, parts and accessories that came along with it
  • We’ll offer your full refund via your original payment method
  • There may be some electronics & other items for which returns may not be possible or a restocking fee may apply. Know more about additional delivery, restocking & other charges on our Delivery FAQ page
Are there any items excluded from the return policy?

The following products and services are non-refundable and non-exchangeable:

  • Special order or customized items (example: Paint, products sold per metre, cut timber
  • Damaged or used items, which are no longer in a sellable condition
  • Items by reason of their nature (video games, DVDs, CDs, bedding); perishable goods (food, drinks, plants, magazines) and personal hygiene products (shavers, toothbrush and headphones)
  • Installed and assembled items, except for faulty items
  • Delivery charges, except for faulty item
How do I return my items?

If you’re unhappy with your item and would like to return, exchange or refund it, we’ve made it as easy as possible.

What should I do if my order arrives damaged, defective or if the wrong item was delivered?

If you notice any visible damage to the item on delivery, refuse the delivery and call us at 800 ASK ACE (800 275 223). If you find the item is damaged after you’ve opened it, immediately return it to your nearest ACE store or call us to arrange a pick-up of your damaged item(s).

Can I return an item that was purchased under promotion or came with a bundle deal?

You can return promotional or bundled items, but you’ll also have to return any free or discounted product that came along with the original item. We don't offer a partial refund on a promotional bundle.
